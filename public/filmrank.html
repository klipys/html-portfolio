My top 3

The green mile

The story revolves around Paul Edgecomb, an aging prison guard in Louisiana during the 1930s. The film explores themes of justice, compassion, and supernatural elements. The “Green Mile” refers to the path that leads condemned prisoners to the execution chamber.

Great movie

Seven

Seven (sometimes stylized as “Se7en”) is a psychological thriller film directed by David Fincher and released in 1995. The movie stars Brad Pitt and Morgan Freeman as detectives David Mills and William Somerset, respectively

Thriller best Movie

La Casa de Papel

The series revolves around a mysterious man known as “The Professor” who plans the most audacious heist ever attempted. To execute his plan, he recruits eight of Spain’s best criminals, each adopting a city name as their pseudonym: Tokyo, Rio, Berlin, Denver, Nairobi, Oslo, Helsinki, and Moscow. Their goal is to infiltrate the Royal Mint of Spain and print €2.4 billion in 50 euro notes within eleven days, all while avoiding casualties among the 67 hostages, including the daughter of the UK ambassador

Best Series
